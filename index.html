Visualizing UKY ARMY ROTC

The slideshow above is an interactive map that explores Hisle Farm Park!

Description

Hisle Farm Park is utlized by UKY ROTC to conduct military lab exercises. We conduct mission operations, land navigation and readiness training at this 280 acre park located just outside Lexington.

The goal of this project is to raise awarness of Hisle Farm Park for future Army Offciers that go through the Wildcat Battalion. This website is for the betterment of the program and should be used to help bring UKY ROTC to life using GIS.

Visualizations created from lidar data provided by KyFromAbove in ArcGIS Pro and Blender. Additional sources of information from Kyraster.gov, April, 2023.

Page and visualizations created by Cadet Jack Chappuies for GEO 409, Department of Geography, University of Kentucky. Spring 2023.
